### Why Humans contract

Humans as often need resources and especially coal, iron, and gems are found underground and remembering they try to dodge the underworld - the land without return - they go for the nine hells and the abyss to get such resources. As devils do often contracts and therefore are more reliable most humans go for nine hells making them good proceeded. Down there you find streets, bridges, but also always fewer resources. So some brave miners engage adventurers to defend them if a demon is attacking as they will go for the ores. 

As world-builder I would actually do a twist here. Abyss got the property to always change the shape. This is like producing new resources, but Nine hells do not shape (anymore). I would say they just used their strength to prevent it from shaping and now together with all that humans buildings in hells like bridges it nearly stopped shaping at all, so ore regeneration is really slow. 

But not only the Ground brings resources: Also the celestials got a home: Mount celesta. So also those are important to the humans, and they come and for a price they get the right to gather those.
