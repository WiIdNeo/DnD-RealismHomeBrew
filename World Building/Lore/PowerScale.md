### How this scale works

This scale works all about Level compaired to a player's character. Level in this case means your chance to success about one of these in 1 vs 1 is about 1:1 ratio.

# Sorting the scale

## Normal human

This does not mean a normal human but all playable character classes on world-living NPC level. Those NPCs are even below Level 1, as they did not learn to block or any moves a lvl 1 character knows.

So the scale $0 \to 1$ is all about the actual process of Leveling your character to level 1 and all the entities you leave behind on doing so.

So in this scale you find average humans and all animals a normal human can beat: house cats, fish, rats, ... Dogs or wild cats I do not include here, as if they would fight on live or die such an oponent is often really hard for a base human, even if weight is a real bonus here.

On first few levels so like $1 \to 5$ you find many more skilled humans, like thieves, low tier mages and basic soldiers, and next levels of animals. As soon as you learned to block or dodge you can fight dogs and wolves, but als most other beasts. Only in one be one really strong animals like bears or cougars could exceed this scale based on experience in fighting. But you do not only find animals, but also the lowest tier of demons.

And after this in levels of $5 \to 12$ you normally let behind all other humans. This means many priests, mages, elite soldiers and many more. Only the strongest clerics and mages or true heros exceed this scale on their own.

## Demons

Demons have a far scale of strength with their weakest just about normal humans and their strongest being close to god tiers on their own. but they normally it is only possible to true and mighty heros being often seen as half gods in one vs one.

## Dragons

As strongest natural beings on earth, as walking catastrophes of nature itself they ofter reach god tiers with elder. There are only very few beings like actual gods can kill a dragon on their own in a fair 1 vs 1. But as elder for a dragon means many thousands or ten-thousand years it is not often someone reaches this power. Especially because it is a famous quest for gods and adventures to kill dragons and many myths about the power gained by eating it's heart they're hunted often and realy reaching the age of eldery and gain the experience it's need to become so strong.

## Even more powerful beings

There are actually more powerful beings on earth than dragons, but they are really rarely a natural but often created life. Example is the Terasque: As entrophy of anger and the wish of destruction of the titans it is way below their scale but can easily handle a god. This monster is rarely seen and more rarely overcome. To kill such a monster often gods fight together to defend their prayers.

### Titans

As mentioned the titans even exceed the gods and of course the taresque, but they are mysterious beings of time even before the dragons and also the dragons did see them rarely. Scholars think they mostly leaved material plane as they don't like life as humans or even dragons got. They just returned to their primordial planes of elements and lay there in deep slumber, but there are others believing the titans where infected by life as it came to world and therefore died. They do not knwo where but they think not all titans got back to the elemental planes and died on earth. But they also think the death of a titan does not mean a real end for them. They say Elementals emerged from their bodies sparked by the live affected the titans. 

## Gods

I now often talked on the god tier. This Tier is just a different scale extending the normal lvl 20 cap for gods, as they can not be shown on that mortal scale. It is also hard to even compare those and because of this the god scale is made from tiers being tier one the lowest and tier 5 the highest, even if all tier 5 gods or beings are dead or not seen for so many years only some priests and keepers of stories have heard of them.
