## Acid Splash

*Conjuration cantrip* — Player's Handbook

**Casting Time:** 1 action · **Range:** 60 feet · **Components:** V, S · **Duration:** Instantaneous

**Classes:** Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard

You hurl a bubble of acid at one creature, or at two creatures standing within 5 feet of each other. Each target must make a Dexterity save or take 1d6 acid damage.

*Higher Levels:* Damage rises to 2d6 at level 5, 3d6 at 11, and 4d6 at 17.

## Booming Blade

*Evocation cantrip* — Tasha's Cauldron of Everything

**Casting Time:** 1 action · **Range:** Self (5-foot radius) · **Components:** S, M (a melee weapon worth ≥1 sp) · **Duration:** 1 round

**Classes:** Artificer; optional for Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

You strike an adjacent creature with your weapon as a normal melee attack, and on a hit it becomes wreathed in booming energy until the start of your next turn. If it moves willingly before then, it takes 1d8 thunder damage and the effect ends.

*Higher Levels:* At 5th level the melee hit deals +1d8 thunder damage and the movement damage becomes 2d8; both increase again at 11th (2d8/3d8) and 17th (3d8/4d8).

## Chill Touch

*Necromancy cantrip* — Player's Handbook

**Casting Time:** 1 action · **Range:** 120 feet · **Components:** V, S · **Duration:** 1 round

**Classes:** Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

A ghostly skeletal hand attacks a creature with a ranged spell attack. On a hit it deals 1d8 necrotic damage and the target can't regain hit points until the start of your next turn. If the target is undead, it also has disadvantage on attacks against you until your next turn ends.

*Higher Levels:* Damage rises to 2d8 at level 5, 3d8 at 11, 4d8 at 17.

## Control Flames

*Transmutation cantrip* — Xanathar's Guide to Everything

**Casting Time:** 1 action · **Range:** 60 feet · **Components:** S · **Duration:** Instantaneous or 1 hour

**Classes:** Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard

You manipulate a nonmagical flame in a 5-foot cube you can see: expand it 5 feet (if fuel is present), snuff it out, alter the light it casts (doubling/halving radius, changing color, both — for 1 hour), or shape it into simple animated forms for 1 hour. Up to three non-instantaneous effects can be active at once; you can dismiss one as an action.

## Encode Thoughts

*Enchantment cantrip* — Guildmaster's Guide to Ravnica

**Casting Time:** 1 action · **Range:** Self · **Components:** S · **Duration:** 8 hours

**Classes:** None (no default class list)



You draw a memory, idea, or message from your mind and turn it into a small glowing "thought strand" object that can be carried. If you're concentrating on a thought-reading effect (such as *detect thoughts* or *modify memory*) when you cast this, you can encode someone else's read thoughts instead of your own. Casting the spell again while holding a strand (or casting *detect thoughts* on it) instantly delivers its contents to you.
