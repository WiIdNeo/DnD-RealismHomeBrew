# HP

HP, so the real hits you can take until unconsious or death, do not really scale so much over live.

This looks my formula like atm:

$$\text{HP} = 50 + \text{CON-Mod}\times3$$

## 0 HP

0 HP does mean you are unconscious. If damage exceeds this lethally your dm may decides to count it as death.
