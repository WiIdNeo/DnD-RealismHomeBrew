As already hovered on, the levels in my system are representation of skill and might. Because of this the levels mean actual progress in experience, so fighting always the same or unskilled entities grant you less XP. 

So as higher you proceed in levels as lower XP you gain. A possible form could be:

$$\text{Group XP} = 2\times\text{Number of Players}\times\frac{Enemy's XP}{(\text{Player's Level}/\text{(Enemy's CR - Pre Fight Knowledge of the Enemy)})}$$

This makes leveling on lower levels a little faster and later a little slower, showing the faster learning curve of the beginner and the plateau you reach later on. But also the grid of levels may need a rework than. This needs some play testing.
