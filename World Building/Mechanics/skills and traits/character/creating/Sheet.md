## We drop Alignment, Bounds and Flaws

Alignments are not really useful during Sessions. Also just 9 Possibillities are to flat. So we drop this. Same for Bounds and Flaws, they are just not important or useful for normal player.

Instead we will create an cataloge of questions in advance to make the player think about how the character is. Why is he doing what? That's a bit closer how actors do it, and as we also act/play a character here, that's just better for real emotion.

Also we recommend to not write those questions to the main Sheet. I would more have it on an extra sheet in case you become unsure about how your character would react, but more important if DM or another player takes your character for some reason.

You can do this question thing multiple times as well and than you will may notice some differences after some seassions.
