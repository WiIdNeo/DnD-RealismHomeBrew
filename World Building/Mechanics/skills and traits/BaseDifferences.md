# HP and Stamina
## Heal
Most noticeable Difference is the split of DnD's TP to TP and Stamina. This got a rat tail: Is healing now applied to stamina or health?
Basically you can work by following rule: Do you imagine the cure being renewing (like a fresh wind (second wind)) or increasing your clearness in fight than it's stamina, if it is clearly healing your wounds (cure wounds) than it's your HP. If it is not clearly visible just split both. Personal I would split 2/3 Stamina and 1/3 HP, as Stamina is for fight more important, but that's DM flavor.

Of course I will go over all classes and skills and traits to define that for this system, but it will take me a long while, so may just work with that rules as long it's needed.
